2 (A1) to GND
3 (A2) to GND
4  (GND) to GND
5  (SDA) to A4
6 (SCL) to A5
7  (WP) to GND
8 (VCC) to 5V

connect a 4.7k resistor from 5 to 5v and from 6 to 5v. 

Open file in Arduino IDE and run.
